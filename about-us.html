About Us

We are a second-hand book trading website that provides a convenient platform for buyers and sellers to connect and trade books.

At our website, we are committed to providing a secure and user-friendly platform for all our users. We understand that buying and selling second-hand books can be a daunting task, which is why we have made it our mission to simplify the process and make it as seamless as possible.

It is important to note that we charge a 5% service fee on every completed transaction. This fee helps us maintain and improve our website, and ensures that we continue to provide our users with the best possible experience.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@secondhandbooktrading.com. We are always happy to hear from our users and provide any assistance that we can.
